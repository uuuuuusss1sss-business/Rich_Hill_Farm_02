About Our Farm

Welcome to Rich Hill Farm, a family-owned farm dedicated to growing the most delicious peaches. We take pride in our sustainable farming practices and our commitment to quality.

Our peach season typically runs from July to August. Come and visit us to pick your own fresh peaches, or buy them pre-picked at our farm stand. Enjoy a fun day out with the family and experience the joy of picking your own fruit.
